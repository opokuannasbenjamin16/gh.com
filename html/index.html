REASONS WHY GHANA'S OFFICIAL LANGUAGE IS ENGLISH

  1. BRITISH COLONIALISM: Ghana was a British colony from 1821 to 1957. During this period ,English was imposed as the language of adminstration,education and commerce.
  2. EDUCATION: English was the language of instruction in schools and universities during the colonial era and this continued after independence.
  3. NATIONAL UNITY: With over 100 local languages spoken in Ghana, English serves as lingua franca, helping to unify the country and facilitate communication among the people from different regions.
